Our Story

Welcome to ZAD Cafe, a cozy corner in the heart of Addis Ababa where flavor meets warmth. Since 2020, we have been serving handcrafted dishes that celebrate both local Ethiopian tastes and international favorites.

From our signature Chechebsa to our freshly baked pizzas, every item on our menu is prepared with love and the finest ingredients. Whether you are here for a morning coffee, a business lunch, or a family dinner, we make sure you leave with a smile.
